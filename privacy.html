TipidMix — Privacy Policy

Effective: 7 June 2026 · App: TipidMix (ph.tipidmix.app) · Publisher: MAK-CT Construction OPC

The short version

TipidMix works fully offline. Your projects, pours, plants, batches, and prices stay in your phone's local storage. We cannot see them. There is no account, no sign-up, and no upload of your job data.

Data stored on your device only

  • Projects, pours, custom mixes, plant levels, batch tickets, receipts, price book, settings — stored in your browser/app local database (IndexedDB). Deleting the app (or its data) deletes them. The backup file you can export is saved where you choose and never sent to us.

Data you may choose to send us

  • Feedback: if you tap Send in More ▸ Feedback, we receive your message plus anonymous technical context (app version, language, device model string, screen size, record counts, recent error messages). No names, no contact details unless you type them, no job data.
  • Error reports: if "auto-send error reports" is on (you can turn it off in More), the same anonymous technical context may be sent when the app hits an error, so repeat crashes get fixed fast.

Advertising (free tier)

The free Android app shows ads via Google AdMob. AdMob may process your device's advertising identifier and related signals to serve and measure ads, as described in Google's Privacy Policy. You can reset or delete your advertising ID in Android settings. TipidMix Pro removes ads. The web (PWA) version currently shows no ads.

What we never do

  • No sale of personal data. No contact scraping. No location tracking. No analytics SDKs beyond the ad network in the free Android tier.

Data Privacy Act (PH)

We follow the Philippine Data Privacy Act of 2012 (RA 10173). Because your job data never leaves your device, TipidMix processes no personal data server-side except the optional feedback described above, which is used solely to improve the app and is deleted when no longer needed.

Children

TipidMix is a professional construction tool, not directed at children under 13.
